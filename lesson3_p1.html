Common Cyber Attacks

Part 1: Threat Landscape

💡 Introduction:

In our interconnected world, cyber attacks have become a constant threat. These are deliberate attempts by unauthorized users to gain access to digital systems, steal sensitive data, or disrupt essential services.

Attack Types

  • Phishing Attacks
    • Sends deceptive emails to steal sensitive login credentials.
    • Often mimics trusted banks or social media platforms.
  • Malware Threats
    • Malicious software designed to damage or disable computers.
    • Includes various types like viruses, worms, and spyware.
  • Ransomware
    • Encrypts user files and demands payment for the decryption key.
    • Can cause permanent data loss if backups are not available.
  • DoS Attacks
    • Floods systems with traffic to make them unavailable to users.

Threat Landscape: Identifying common digital attack vectors, from deceptive social engineering to destructive malicious software and data-locking ransomware.
