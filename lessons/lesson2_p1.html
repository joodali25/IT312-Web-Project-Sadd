Risk Management

Part 2: Risk Response & Tips

Risk Response Strategies Summary

Strategy Description Example
Risk Avoidance Eliminate the risk entirely Not starting a risky project
Risk Mitigation Reduce the impact or likelihood Installing a firewall
Risk Transfer Shift the risk to a third party Purchasing insurance
Risk Acceptance Acknowledge and proceed Accepting minor bugs
Risk Assessment Analyze and prioritize risks Using a risk matrix
